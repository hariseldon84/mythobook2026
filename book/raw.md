# Kurukshetra: Error 404
*(Alternative Titles: Hastinapur Heights, The Noida Yuga, Mahabharata 2.0: The Glitch, Pandavas & Co.)*

## 📖 Plot Introduction
Rahul Sharma, 34, a mildly useless software engineer from Noida, is test-driving his eccentric uncle’s time machine—a customized Maruti 800 running on two Amazon Alexas and a JioFiber router. There is a glitch. Rahul lands in Hastinapur, 3200 BCE, right as the Pandavas and Kauravas are returning from Dronacharya's Gurukul. There’s a side effect to this temporal jump: because Rahul’s biological age can only increment when the calendar switches from 2026 to 2027, and he is stuck in BCE, he effectively stops aging. He becomes the perpetually 34-year-old millennial intern of the Mahabharata.

Rahul stumbles into the grand arena where the princes are showcasing their martial skills. Immediately, he realizes history is *wildly* different from the mythological TV serials. He befriends Sahadeva, who possesses the curse of knowing the future but nobody listens to him. Sahadeva is a deeply depressed, misunderstood psychic who spends his days humming Mohammed Rafi songs in his head. 

Rahul tries to prevent the great war, but realizes the biggest obstacle isn't the rivalry between the cousins—it's **Vyasa**, a struggling author working for *Ganesha Media Ltd.* who desperately needs a bloody, epic war to secure his publishing legacy. Rahul must navigate a corporate Shakuni, an Italian-styled Indraprastha, a PR-driven Draupadi rumor, and random check-ins from a very chill, all-knowing Krishna, all to stop the world's greatest epic from becoming the world's most unnecessary bloodbath.

---

## 🎭 The Cast of Characters
*   **Rahul Sharma:** Protagonist. Useless, tired, zero survival skills, completely immune to aging.
*   **Sahadeva:** Rahul’s best friend. Sees the future, knows Bollywood lyrics, massively depressed because nobody takes his prophecies seriously. 
*   **Shakuni:** Holds a post-graduate certificate from the "Drona Institute of Awesomeness." An opportunistic corporate shark who wants to use Rahul to launch "Mama Ke Nushkhe"—an ancient fin-fluencer YouTube channel—and "MBHT (Mera Bachcha Hai Tu) Media" for political digital marketing.
*   **Vyasa:** The antagonist. A frustrated official war scribe working at Ganesha Media Ltd. who desperately needs a dramatic war for his upcoming book to sell.
*   **Lord Ganesha:** Reclusive CEO of Ganesha Media Ltd., lives in Kailasha Mansion, possesses cross-timeline tech, and only grants meetings during ribbon-cutting inaugurations.
*   **Draupadi / Panchali:** Only married to Arjuna. She functions as the brilliant political counsel for all five brothers. Vyasa maliciously spread the "married to five men" rumor purely for literary shock value and PR.
*   **Krishna (Madhava):** Smirking, omniscient, completely unbothered. Casually drops by and knows all of Rahul’s embarrassing childhood secrets.
