# Chapter 13: The Great Noida Restoration

If I wanted to blend in, all I had to do was wear the dark green Kaurava silks, aggressively stroke my fake beard, and occasionally yell about taxation policies whenever anyone made eye contact with me. 

Within three weeks, Prince Rahala had accidentally become King Dhritarashtra's absolute favorite son. 

It wasn't because of my combat skills—which were entirely nonexistent—or my mythological pedigree. It was purely because I was the only person in the entire Bronze Age palace who understood basic comedic timing.
